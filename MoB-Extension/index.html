- INFORMATION -

The Information is where you can observe the progress of your segmentation, it has details of the blocks made, or the params used such as the granularity.
The New block action is used to create new blocks. Just click the button to activate it and click a section on the page to create the new block. You can guide yourself using the highlight guide.
The Delete block action is used to delete a block. Just click the button to activate it and click on the blocks you want to delete.
The Merge block action is used to combine two blocks into one. Just click the button to activate it and then click the two blocks blocks you want to combine.
The Cut block action is used to separate two blocks that are overlapping eachother. Just click the button to activate it, then, click the block you want to "preservate" over the other and later, click the second block (this one will be cut).
The Tag block action is used to add a tag to a block. Just click the button to activate it, then click the block you want to identify and choose from the list of tags.
The Select block action is used to show a block's details. Just click the button to activate it, then click any block to show its details.
The Submit segmentation action is used to send your segmentation details to the server. Just click the button to send the segmentation, if there's any error an alert should popup.

For more information, visit the official MoB Repository website
